üìùüìå Important Logistical Notes

  • Transport: Purchase a JR Pass (Whole Japan) or use IC cards (Suica/Pasmo) for local transit. The Shinkansen is the fastest way between cities.
  • Internet: Rent a Pocket Wi-Fi or buy an eSim (Ubigi/Airalo) at the airport for seamless navigation via Google Maps.
  • Luggage: Use Takkyubin (luggage forwarding service) between hotels to travel hands-free on trains.
  • Cash: While cards are becoming common, carry cash (Yen) for small shrines, markets, and some rural shops.
  • Etiquette: Observe silence on public transport and follow strict trash disposal rules.
