ðŸ“ðŸ“Œ Important Logistical Notes

  • Transport: Rent a private car with a driver for mainland Bali (Ubud/Munduk); it's affordable and efficient.
  • Fast Boats: Book Gili and Nusa Penida boats in advance; expect choppy waters in May.
  • Connectivity: Grab a Telkomsel SIM at the airport or use an eSIM (Airalo/Holafly) for reliable data.
  • Cash/Apps: Download Grab or Gojek for transport and food delivery. Most cafes take cards, but keep IDR for local markets.
  • Temple Etiquette: Always wear a sarong (usually provided/rented at the entrance) when visiting temples.
